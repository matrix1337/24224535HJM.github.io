Albums

Cry for the Infinity Catalog: CYGN-0000

My 1st album, it contains 13 songs that have an intense and messy atmosphere. That wasn't the intention, it was just the first work published in my circle and as a result it's just not very good.

The Evolution of War Catalog: CYGN-0001

The 2nd album in the 蛾喰い music circle. The compositions are vastly superior to those on Cry for the Infinity, but the mixing and mastering could have been much better. It contains 20 songs.

逢魔樹 Catalog: CYGN-XXXX

My 3rd album, this one was never physically produced. This is why it doesn't have a catalog number. It contains an intense gothic atmosphere and all of the works are fairly decent compositions with mediocre mixing. It contains 12 songs.

Midnight Sacrifice Catalog: CYGN-0002

My 4th album. I consider this to be my first good work, it was a collaboration album with GOETIA. It contains 20 compositions, 3 of which were by GOETIA.

Sorrow Mania Catalog: CYGN-0003

My 5th album. It contains 18 songs. The compositions emit a strange feeling, much of the choirs are heavily chorused and have an unusual texture to them.

Lament Catalog: CYGN-0004

My 6th album. The kick and bass are mixed to be very hard hitting, plenty of arpeggios and tense gothic melodies that give a sense of unease to the listener.
